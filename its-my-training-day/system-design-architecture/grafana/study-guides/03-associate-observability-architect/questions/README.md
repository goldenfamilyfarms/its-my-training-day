## How to Use These Questions

### Self-Assessment

1. **Read the question** - Understand what's being asked
2. **Formulate your answer** - Write or speak your response before looking at the answer
3. **Compare** - Review the provided answer and identify gaps
4. **Deep dive** - Research areas where your knowledge is weak

### Mock Interview Practice

1. Have someone ask you the questions randomly
2. Practice explaining concepts verbally
3. Time yourself - aim for 3-5 minutes per answer
4. Get feedback on clarity and completeness

### Study Strategy

| Phase | Approach |
|-------|----------|
| **First Pass** | Answer all questions, note weak areas |
| **Deep Study** | Focus on topics where you struggled |
| **Second Pass** | Re-attempt questions after studying |
| **Final Review** | Quick review of all answers before interview |

## Tips for Success

### For Technical Questions

- **Be specific** - Use concrete examples and real scenarios
- **Show your process** - Explain how you approach problems
- **Acknowledge trade-offs** - Discuss pros and cons of approaches
- **Connect to experience** - Relate answers to your background

### For Customer Scenario Questions

- **Focus on communication** - How you explain things matters
- **Show empathy** - Understand the customer's perspective
- **Be structured** - Use frameworks for problem-solving
- **Demonstrate follow-through** - Explain how you ensure resolution

### For Documentation Questions

- **Know your audience** - Different docs for different readers
- **Be practical** - Focus on actionable content
- **Show examples** - Reference documentation you've created
- **Discuss maintenance** - How to keep docs current
